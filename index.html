What is a fuzz

A Fuzz pedal takes the signal from and adds harmonic overtones into the upper frequency range turning the singal nearly into a square wave. The Fuzz sound is often described by the extreme clipping you can hear in the overtones which is when all of the frequency range is peaking giving a very distorted crunchy sound that dominates the signal.
