The Tone Bender

In 1965 Gary Hurst (previously employed at VOX one of the largest amplifier manufactuers) created the Sola Sound Tone Bender MK1. This pedal was influenced by the Marstro FZ-1 Fuzz Tone but used three germanium transitors to get that gritty punch and sustain the Fuzz is known for.

The Tone Bender has various iterations throughout years that inspired feature circuits in this deep dive. The Yardbirds’ “Heart Full of Soul” displays the qualities this pedal is known for.
